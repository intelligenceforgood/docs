# Why I4G

I4G is a threat intelligence aggregation platform for online fraud. It
ingests scattered victim reports, extracts structured threat entities,
links related cases, and produces intelligence products for law
enforcement and partner organizations.

## The problem

Fraud reporting is fragmented across agencies and jurisdictions. A Bitcoin
wallet reported to the FTC in the US may also appear in reports to Action
Fraud in the UK — but without cross-referencing, each report is treated
as an isolated incident. Scammers exploit this by recycling
infrastructure: the same email addresses, crypto wallets, phone numbers,
and domains appear across dozens of independent reports.

## What I4G does

| Capability                  | Description                                                                                                   |
| --------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Entity extraction**       | Finds wallet addresses, emails, phones, domains, and URLs in freeform reports — including obfuscated variants |
| **Case linking**            | Automatically connects reports that share threat entities                                                     |
| **Analyst review at scale** | Structured engagement model for volunteers, students, and professionals to classify and investigate cases     |
| **Actionable output**       | Signed dossiers, STIX 2.1 bundles, and structured exports for law enforcement and partners                    |

## Who uses I4G

| Role                        | Function                                              |
| --------------------------- | ----------------------------------------------------- |
| **Analysts**                | Review cases, classify fraud, investigate entities    |
| **Engagement participants** | Students in university programs reviewing cases       |
| **Law enforcement**         | Receive dossiers and intelligence feeds               |
| **Reporters**               | Submit fraud reports through the intake form          |
| **Administrators**          | Manage users, engagements, and platform configuration |
