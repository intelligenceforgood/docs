# Platform Overview

Intelligence for Good (i4g) combines human expertise with responsible AI to help communities identify, document, and report online fraud. The platform focuses on **crypto and romance scams**, often impacting seniors and other vulnerable users, but supports anyone who needs help.

## Mission

- Empower users to verify suspicious conversations early and receive actionable guidance.
- Equip volunteer analysts with a secure, collaborative workspace for evidence review.
- Deliver standardized, court-ready reports that accelerate law enforcement investigations.

## Core Capabilities (what users care about)

| Layer | What it does for you |
| --- | --- |
| Intake | Accepts chats, screenshots, receipts; auto-detects language and sentiment; masks PII on upload. |
| Detection | Finds key entities (wallets, emails, phones), classifies scam type, and links related cases. |
| Search & Evidence | Hybrid search (keywords + semantic) across your submissions and similar cases; keeps evidence organized. |
| Human Review | Verified volunteers review masked data, add findings, and prepare reports. |
| Reporting | Generates signed, court-ready dossiers you can share with law enforcement. |
