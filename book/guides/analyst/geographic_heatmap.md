# Geographic Heatmap

The geographic heatmap shows where fraud cases originate, aggregated by victim
country. Use it to identify geographic hotspots and drill into country-level details.

## Country Summary

The main view displays:

- **Countries**: total number of countries represented in the data.
- **Total Cases**: sum of all cases across countries.
- **Total Loss**: aggregate financial loss.

Below the summary cards, countries are ranked by case volume with horizontal
bars showing relative scale.

## Country Drill-Down

Click any country in the list to open a detail panel showing:

- Total case count and loss for that country.
- Individual case records with case ID, classification badge, and loss amount.

Use the close button (×) to dismiss the detail panel.

## Interpreting Hotspots

- Countries with disproportionately high case counts relative to loss may
  indicate high-volume low-value fraud patterns.
- Countries with high loss but low case count may indicate targeted
  high-value schemes.
- Use the period selector to compare geographic patterns across time ranges.

## Period Filter

Adjust the period (30d, 90d, quarter, year) to narrow or expand the time
window. The summary stats, country rankings, and drill-down data all update
to reflect the selected period.
