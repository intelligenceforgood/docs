# Following Up

After you submit a report, here's what happens and how you can check on its
progress.

## What happens to your report

1. **Received** — your report is in the system and waiting in the review
   queue.
2. **In review** — an analyst is reading your report, classifying the scam
   type, and extracting identifiers (wallet addresses, phone numbers, URLs).
3. **Needs more info** — the analyst has a question or needs additional
   evidence. Check your report for a message from the team.
4. **Accepted** — the report has been classified and its entities are being
   linked with other cases. Your information is contributing to intelligence.
5. **Closed** — all actions are complete. The case may have been referred to
   law enforcement or merged into a larger campaign investigation.

## Checking your status

Open the **My Reports** tab after signing in. You'll see a list of your
submissions with their current status.

If the status shows **Needs more info**, open the report to read the
analyst's message. You can reply in the thread or attach new evidence.

## How your report makes a difference

Your report doesn't exist in isolation. The platform links identifiers
across all victim reports — so the wallet address in your report might
connect to dozens of other victims. These connections are what turn
individual fraud reports into actionable intelligence.

When enough cases cluster around the same entities, the platform identifies
a [campaign](../key-concepts/campaigns.md) — a coordinated fraud operation.
Analysts produce [evidence dossiers](../key-concepts/dossiers-and-reports.md)
from these campaigns and deliver them to law enforcement.

Even if your individual case doesn't lead to an arrest, the identifiers
you provided strengthen the overall intelligence picture.

## Getting help

- Use the in-app help link to contact support.
- For urgent safety concerns, notify your liaison immediately — don't wait
  for an in-app reply.
- If you've lost access to your account, contact
  `support@intelligenceforgood.org`.
