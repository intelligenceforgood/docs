# Welcome to i4g

**Intelligence for Good (i4g)** turns scattered fraud reports into coordinated
intelligence that protects real people. Volunteers, university students, law
enforcement, and AI work together to identify scam networks, track threat
actors, and disrupt fraud campaigns before more victims are harmed.

## What you'll find here

This site is your guide to using the i4g platform — whether you're reviewing
cases as an analyst, submitting a report as a victim, managing an engagement
program, or integrating with the API as a partner organization.
