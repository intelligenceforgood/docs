# Start Here

Welcome to the Analyst Guide. This section walks you through every
tool in the I4G Console — from triage to intelligence production.

## Before you begin

- Sign in at [app.intelligenceforgood.org](https://app.intelligenceforgood.org)
  with your authorized Google account.
- Your account must be in the **analyst** role (or higher). If you see
  an access error, ask your program administrator to add you.
- If you are part of a university engagement, select your engagement
  from the sidebar selector before starting work. See
  [Working in an Engagement](../engagement-guide/working-in-engagement.md).

## Best practices

- Keep notes factual and concise. Avoid personally identifying
  language in free-form text fields.
- Use taxonomy classifications consistently — they power the
  analytics that drive grant reporting and law enforcement referrals.
- If a case appears to be part of a larger campaign, link it from the
  [Campaigns](campaigns.md) page.
- Coordinate handoffs in your team channel, especially if you cannot
  finish a review in one session.

## Escalation paths

| Situation                 | Action                                                |
| ------------------------- | ----------------------------------------------------- |
| Urgent safety issue       | Notify the program administrator immediately          |
| Potential law enforcement | Flag with your LEA liaison; provide context           |
| Tooling issue or bug      | Open a ticket with reproduction steps and screenshots |
